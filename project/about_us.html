So... How does this work?

Community members donate gently used toys and homeschool materials to the library. Available materials are catalogued on this website.

Browse to find what you want, and then contact Sarah to reserve the items. You can pick up your reservations from Sarah's house on the following Friday, and return them any day of the week.

The default check-out period is two weeks, although special arrangements can be made if you know you'll need it for longer.

Where is this located?

The library is currently operating out of Sarah's home in West Jordan, Utah. It is near 9000 S and 4800 W.

How do we "contribute?"

Gently used homeschool materials and toys are always appreciated. Some wear is okay, but items should have a decent amount of life left to live. Non-educative board games and puzzles are not currently being accepted, except baby/toddler style puzzles.

Donations of time are also welcome: cleaning toys, photographing new donations, and preparing items that have been reserved for community members to pick up.

Sometimes, neither of the above options work for a family. In that case, a small financial donation can be put toward the needs of the library (storage bins, new items, etc). Sarah's Venmo handle is @S-Woolston. In the transaction description, please include your name, the month, and "for the library."
